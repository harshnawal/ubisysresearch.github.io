Sensepod: AIoT-Based Testbed for Sensing as a Service (SaaS)

Sensepod is an AIoT-based testbed designed to promote Sensing as a Service (SaaS) for ambient living. The platform supports continuous collection and preprocessing of multimodal sensory data within a smart home, tailored for ambient intelligence applications. Its dynamic and flexible approach allows researchers to customize data acquisition to meet specific research objectives.

Key Features

  • Continuous & Multimodal Data Collection: Real-time data from various sensors monitor the environment, occupancy, and user interactions.
  • Application-Centric: Ideal for fire alarm systems, human occupancy detection, and environmental sensing such as air quality, humidity, and lighting.
  • Human-Centric Processing: Captures user activities, aiding research in behavior analysis, smart healthcare, and home automation.
